Synergies & Dissynergies Detail

High-level synergies and potential dissynergies from the integration

High-Level Synergies

Digital Transformation & CRM

VetWell's AI CRM across PetCare: automate scheduling, cut admin workload

Service Expansion & Revenue Growth

Cross-selling premium services, bundled offerings, new subscription models

Operational Efficiency

Centralized procurement, reduced TPP costs via bulk purchasing

Talent Development

Standardized training, AI diagnostics across all clinics

Different cultures & service models — needs change management investment
